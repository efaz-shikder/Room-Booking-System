FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How to sign up:

  1. Input your first and last name.
  2. Input your TDSB email. You cannot register an account if you use an email with any other extension.
  3. Input your password (There is a check box located just above the sign up button that you may click to reveal your password).
  4. Re-enter your password.
  5. Click sign up.
  6. You will receive a confirmation email in the TDSB email you registered with.
  7. After account confirmation the page will redirect to a page identical to the first image.
  8. Your account is now registered.

How to sign in:

  1. You will be taken to the sign in page once you press the “Sign In” button.
  2. Input your email and password
  3. Click Remember Me to save your login information
  4. Click Hide Password to hide your password
  5. Click Forgot Password? if you’ve forgotten your password.
    1. Once clicked, you will receive an email prompting for the change of your password.
    2. Once changed, sign in with your new password.
  6. Press Login if you’ve inputted your email and password correctly.
  7. You will be taken to the homepage.

Room Booking Tutorial

  1. Date selection
    1. The current date is highlighted in dark blue.
    2. The selected date(the date when you wish to book a room) is highlighted in light blue.
    3. The non-bookable dates are greyed out. Such dates include but are not limited to: PA days, weekends, and vacation days.
    4. Note: You may not click a block letter or hallway until this component is completed.
  2. Period selection
    1. The four buttons below the calendar indicate the periods.
    2. Choose the period that you want to book a room for.
    3. The chosen period will be highlighted in dark blue.
    4. If you made a mistake and wish to choose another period, click on the desired period.
    5. Note: You may not click on a hallway until this component is completed.
  3. Hallway selection
    1. After selecting the period and the date, you can now hover over the hallway options.
    2. The hovered hallway will have two blue lines that indicate selection.
    3. When a hallway option is hovered over, the corresponding hallway on the map will illuminate.
    4. Click on the desired hallway and a dropdown menu comprising of all the bookable rooms in that hallway will appear.
    5. Select the room you want to book.
    6. The room selected will be highlighted in dark gray, as shown in the picture.
  4. Submit booking
    1. Once you have a room, a period, and a date selected, you can submit your room booking.
    2. When pressed, an alert will show asking for your confirmation.
    3. Press OK to confirm your booking.
    4. Press Cancel if you want to make alterations to your booking.
  5. View booking
    1. To view your bookings, click on the icon located at the top left of the webpage.
    2. When clicked, a navigation bar will appear.
    3. Click “Booked Rooms” to view your booked rooms.
    4. Below the options “Date, Room Number, Period” will be the details of your booking.
  6. Cancel booking
    1. To cancel a booking. Press the cancel button on the row of the booking that you want to cancel. You will receive a confirmation alert.
    2. After deleting a room, you can return to the homepage to book another room.

User Interface Layout

  • The white icon on the top left of the interface will open a navigation bar when pressed.
  • The calendar shows the current date and is used to select your desired date for booking.
  • The buttons labelled A-D below the calendar shows the periods. Click to select your desired period.
  • The submit button below the periods cannot be clicked until a date, period, and room is chosen.
  • The text Academic Room Booking and Inquiry System will change based on hallway and room hovered.
  • The map is on the lower part of the interface is that of the school. When you hover over the rooms, the text will change to that of its room number, hallway name, and floor. The map cannot be clicked and only serves the purpose of being a tool useful to the user’s navigation.
  • The hallway options on the top part of the interface can only be selected once you choose a desired date and period. Clicking on the text opens a menu of all bookable rooms.
  • The Logout button allows you to log out of the account.

Admin Privileges:

  • Remove other teacher's booked rooms
  • Make rooms unbookable or bookable
  • Create new rooms
  • View the name, email, access level, and the bookability of a teacher
  • Deny a teacher's access to ARBIS
  • Take away a teacher's ability to book rooms
  • Make any teacher an administrator
  • Edit schedule
