    FAQS

  • How Do I Book A Room?

  • In order to book a room, please go to our Booking Page using the ARBIS side menu and click on a hallway in our indicated hallway selection menu. This should open a new compartment just beneath the hallway menu, where you can choose an individual classroom to book in that hallway. Once selected, you will see a confirmation alert to ensure this is the room that you like. Should this hallway not contain your desired room, feel free to click on your desired hallway selection menu. When selecting a hallway, the map will illuminate to reveal which hallway you are about to select.

  • How Can I Cancel a Room that I have booked?

  • Use the ARBIS side menu to go to your Booked Rooms and click on the "delete" button in the settings section directly to the right of the room you wish to reschedule. A popup will appear to confirm your decision.

  • Can I Declare a room as unavailable?

  • To avoid any misuse of this particular privelage, we only give this option to administrative users (principal, vice principal, etc.). A room will only be declared unavailable if it is unsafe to use or under renovations.

  • My Account Is not working.

  • This may be due to the fact that your device is having trouble connecting to our server. Please refresh the page or restart your device. Feel free to contact us should the problem persist. Another factor may be that you have forgotten your password, please click on Forgot Password? and follow the instructions to change your password.

  • What Does ARBIS Stand For?

  • The Name ARBIS stands for: Academic Room Booking and Inquiry System. This name comes from the fact that the system is specifically designed for the purpose of booking rooms for academic use within Victoria Park. It is also for the purpose of inquiring whether a room is available and if not, when the room will be available next.
