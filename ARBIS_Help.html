FAQS

  • How Do I Book A Room?

  • In order to book a room, please go to our Booking Page using the ARBIS side menu and click on a hallway in our indicated hallway selection menu. This should bring you to a new selection menu where you can choose an individual classroom to book in that hallway. Should this hallway not contain your desired room, feel free to click on the back button in order to return to the hallway selection menu.

  • How Do I Know if a Room is unavailable?

  • The room name would be grayed out and the booking button text, "book" will be replaced by "unavailable" or "Booked by: 'the name of the teacher'" depending on the reason the room is unavailable.

  • How Can I Reschedule a booked room?

  • Use the ARBIS side menu to go to your Booked Rooms and click on the "edit" button in the settings section directly to the right of the room you wish to reschedule. A popup will appear to confirm your decision.

  • How Can I Cancel a Room that I have booked?

  • Use the ARBIS side menu to go to your Booked Rooms and click on the "delete" button in the settings section directly to the right of the room you wish to reschedule. A popup will appear to confirm your decision.

  • Can I Declare a room as unavailable?

  • To avoid any misuse of this particular privelage, we only give this option to administrative users (principal, vice principal, etc.).

  • What Does ARBIS Stand For?

  • The Name ARBIS stands for: Academic Room Booking and Inquiry System. This name comes from the fact that the system is specifically designed for the purpose of booking rooms for academic use within Victoria Park. It is also for the purpose of inquiring whether a room is available and if not, when the room will be available next.
