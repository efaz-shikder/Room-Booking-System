FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Admin Privileges:

  • Remove other teacher's booked rooms
  • Make rooms unbookable or bookable
  • Create new rooms
  • View the name, email, access level, and the bookability of a teacher
  • Deny a teacher's access to ARBIS
  • Take away a teacher's ability to book rooms
  • Make any teacher an administrator

How to remove a teacher's booked room:

  1. Click open the side navigation and go to all booked rooms.
  2. You will find a list of rooms arranged in chronological order.
  3. If you are looking for a room on a certain date, you can search the date in the search bar located above the table.
  4. Click the red cancel button on the row of the room you want to cancel.
  5. An alert will pop up asking for your confirmation.
  6. If you pressed OK, the room is now cancelled and free to be booked again.

How to make rooms bookable/unbookable:

  1. Click open the side navigation and go to edit rooms.
  2. A table with the columns"Room name", "Hallway", and "Bookable" will appear.
  3. You can toggle the room's bookability with the switch on the right.

How to make rooms bookable/unbookable:

  1. Click open the side navigation and go to edit rooms.
  2. Located on the right above the table is a button that reads "Add Classroom".
  3. Click the button.
  4. A prompt will appear asking you to fill in the name, select the hallway, and choose the bookability of the classroom.
  5. Press add to add the classroom.
  6. You have added a new classroom.

How to edit teachers:

  1. Click open the side navigation and go to edit teachers.
  2. There is a table with the columns:"First Name", "Last Name", "Email", "Current Level", "Teacher Bookability", and "Make Admin".
  3. The table consists of all of the registered accounts.
  • You can allow and disallow teachers to book rooms with the "Yes" and "No" buttons.
  • You can deny a teacher's ability to view the website with "Deny Access".
  • Pressing "Yes" for an admin will make them a normal teacher.
  • The "Current Level" column indicates the access level of an account.
